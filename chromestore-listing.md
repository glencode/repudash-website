## Detailed Description

Stop agonizing over how to respond to customer reviews. RepuDash is an AI-powered assistant that drafts the perfect, personalized reply directly inside Google Maps, Yelp, and Tripadvisor. 

⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ Rated by local business owners as the #1 tool for review management.

Whether you're managing a bustling restaurant, a retail store, or a service business, tracking and responding to reviews is crucial for local SEO. RepuDash makes it effortless. 

### ✨ Core Features (Free Forever)
- **One-Click Replies:** A "Reply with AI" button is injected natively below every review. No more copy-pasting back and forth.
- **Tone Control:** Choose from Professional, Casual, Friendly, or Empathetic tones.
- **Adjustable Length:** Generate short thank-you's or detailed, empathetic responses to complex feedback.
- **Your Own API Key:** Completely free to use with your own Google Gemini API key. No monthly subscriptions.

### 🚀 Pro & Business Features (One-Time Payment)
- **Multi-Platform:** Support for Yelp and Tripadvisor in addition to Google Maps.
- **Auto-Translation:** Instantly reply to international customers in their native language (Spanish, French, German, Italian, etc.).
- **Batch Processing:** Select up to 10 reviews at once and generate all replies in seconds. 
- **Custom Templates:** Save your own standardized responses and insert them with a click.
- **Competitor Tracking:** Silently track the ratings and review counts of your local competitors right from your dashboard.
- **Analytics & QR Codes:** Generate printable Google Review QR codes and track your rating trends over time.

### 🔒 Privacy First
RepuDash respects your data. We do NOT collect your personal information, browse history, or API keys. All settings, review history, and templates are stored strictly local to your device. 

### 💡 How it works
1. Install RepuDash and enter your free Google Gemini API key.
2. Tell RepuDash your business name and industry.
3. Open a review on Google Business Profile, Yelp, or Tripadvisor.
4. Click "Reply with AI" and watch the magic happen.
