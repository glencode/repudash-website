RepuDash — Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 17, 2026

1. Overview

RepuDash ("the Extension") is an AI-powered review and reputation management tool for Google Maps, Yelp, and TripAdvisor. This policy explains what data the Extension collects, how it is used, and your rights regarding that data.

2. Data We Collect

RepuDash collects and stores the following data locally on your device only (using Chrome's chrome.storage.local API):

  • Settings: Your business name, industry, preferred tone, and notification preferences.
  • API Key: Your Gemini API key (stored locally, never transmitted to our servers).
  • License Key: Your Gumroad license key for tier verification.
  • Review History: Reviews you've generated AI responses for, along with the generated replies.
  • Competitor Data: Business names, ratings, and review counts of competitors you choose to track.
  • Usage Statistics: Daily reply counts for quota management.

3. Data We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect personal information such as your name, email, or browsing history.
  • We do not track your activity across websites.
  • We do not sell, share, or transmit your data to any third party (except as noted in Section 4).
  • We do not run analytics, telemetry, or tracking scripts.

4. Third-Party Services

The Extension communicates with the following external services:

  • Google Gemini API (generativelanguage.googleapis.com): Your review text is sent to this API to generate AI-powered responses. This is done using your own API key. See Google's AI Terms.
  • Gumroad License API (api.gumroad.com): Your license key is verified against Gumroad's servers to validate your subscription tier. No personal data is sent beyond the license key itself.

5. Data Storage & Security

All data is stored locally on your device using Chrome's built-in chrome.storage.local API. No data is stored on external servers controlled by RepuDash. Your Gemini API key is stored in your browser's local storage and is only ever sent to Google's API endpoint.

6. Permissions Explained

  • storage: To save your settings, review history, and usage data locally.
  • activeTab: To read review content on the current page for AI reply generation.
  • sidePanel: To display the RepuDash dashboard panel alongside review pages.
  • notifications: To alert you when new reviews are detected.
  • alarms: To periodically check for new reviews and re-validate your license.
  • downloads: To allow exporting QR codes and analytics reports.
  • Host permissions (Google Maps, Yelp, TripAdvisor): To inject AI reply buttons on review pages.
  • Host permission (Gemini API): To send review text for AI response generation.
  • Host permission (Gumroad API): To verify license keys.

7. Your Rights

  • You can view all stored data at any time via the Extension's Options page.
  • You can delete all stored data by uninstalling the Extension or clearing Chrome's extension data.
  • You can revoke API access by removing your Gemini API key from the settings.

8. Children's Privacy

RepuDash is not intended for use by individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date above.
